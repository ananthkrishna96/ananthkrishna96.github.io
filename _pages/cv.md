## Research profile

I am a PhD Researcher in Mathematical Analysis, Modeling and Applications at SISSA, Trieste, Italy. My research focuses on computational mechanics, reduced-order modeling, scientific computing, scientific machine learning, finite element methods, multiphysics simulations, thermomechanical modeling, parametric PDE systems, uncertainty quantification, optimization, and engineering digital twins.
