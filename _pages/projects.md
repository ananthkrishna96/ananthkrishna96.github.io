## Reduced-order modeling for orthotropic plates and shells

Development of high-fidelity finite element models and reduced-order models for orthotropic plate and shell systems on elastic foundations, including unilateral contact and panel-interface coupling. The work includes intrusive POD-Galerkin methods, data-driven ROMs, POD-NN models, autoencoder latent models, and ML-based coefficient prediction for fast many-query simulation.

**Keywords:** computational mechanics, plate and shell models, finite element methods, reduced-order modeling, POD-Galerkin, scientific machine learning, parametric PDEs.

## Scientific machine learning surrogates for many-query simulation

Development of data-driven and hybrid surrogate modeling workflows for fast approximation of high-fidelity simulation outputs. This includes neural-network surrogates, autoencoder-based compression, ML-based coefficient prediction, and reduced latent representations for parametric PDE systems.

**Keywords:** scientific machine learning, neural-network surrogates, autoencoders, data-driven modeling, reduced-order modeling, optimization.

## Aeroacoustic transfer matrix modeling using CFD and system identification

Master's thesis work on the characterization of aeroacoustic transfer matrices for narrow ducts and perforated plates using compressible CFD and system identification. The project involved CFD simulation, broadband acoustic excitation, post-processing, low-order acoustic network modeling, and data-driven analysis of turbulent flows.

**Keywords:** CFD, aeroacoustics, AVBP, system identification, turbulent flows, reduced-order modeling, MATLAB.

## Physics-based dynamic modeling and validation

Portfolio-project work on physics-based dynamic modeling, including collector-field dynamics, 1D pipe heat-loss modeling, lumped 0D heat-exchanger modeling, coupled forward simulation, validation with operational data, sensor mapping, automated tests, and diagnostic plots.

**Keywords:** scientific computing, ODE models, dynamic simulation, validation, Python, numerical algorithms.

## Multiphysics simulation and parametric optimization

Engineering simulation and numerical modeling work for multi-scale coupled-physics simulations in additive manufacturing of mechanical and electronic systems. The work involved FEA/CFD modeling, multi-objective parametric optimization, and integration of advanced numerical models into simulation codebases.

**Keywords:** FEA, CFD, coupled-physics simulation, additive manufacturing, parametric optimization, C++.

## Aerospace engineering student projects

Earlier aerospace engineering projects included design and analysis of a 6U HyonoSat CubeSat OBDH and thermal subsystem, design and fabrication of a surveillance hovercraft, remote-controlled weather monitoring and surveillance UAV, and free-convection heat-transfer analysis of a thermal flask.

**Keywords:** aerospace engineering, CubeSat, thermal subsystem, UAV, hovercraft, heat transfer.
