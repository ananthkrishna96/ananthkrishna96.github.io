Development of high-fidelity finite element models and reduced-order models for orthotropic plate and shell systems on elastic foundations, including unilateral contact and panel-interface coupling.

The work includes intrusive POD-Galerkin reduction, data-driven ROMs, POD-NN models, autoencoder latent models, and ML-based coefficient prediction for fast many-query simulation.
