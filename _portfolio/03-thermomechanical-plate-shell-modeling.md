This project develops thermomechanical and transient dynamic extensions for structural response prediction, coupled simulation, verification, and reduced-order-modeling workflows.

The objective is to connect high-fidelity computational mechanics models with efficient reduced-order representations suitable for repeated simulation, parameter studies, and engineering design exploration.

**Keywords:** thermomechanics, transient dynamics, structural mechanics, finite elements, reduced-order modeling, coupled simulation.
