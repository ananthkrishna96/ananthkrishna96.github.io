This project develops high-fidelity fourth-order finite element models and reduced-order models for orthotropic plate and shell systems on elastic foundations, including unilateral contact and panel-interface coupling.

The work includes intrusive POD-Galerkin methods, data-driven reduced-order models, POD-NN models, neural-network surrogates, autoencoder latent models, and ML-based coefficient prediction for fast many-query simulation.

**Keywords:** computational mechanics, finite element methods, plate and shell models, elastic foundations, interface coupling, contact, POD-Galerkin, non-intrusive ROM, scientific machine learning.
