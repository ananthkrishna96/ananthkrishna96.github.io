This work involved FEA and CFD modeling for multi-scale coupled-physics simulations in additive manufacturing of mechanical and electronic systems.

The work included multi-objective parametric optimization algorithms, simulation efficiency improvements, design exploration, and integration of numerical models into engineering analysis codebases.

**Keywords:** CFD, FEA, coupled physics, additive manufacturing, parametric optimization, numerical algorithms, C++.
